About KLU ONE

KLU ONE is a comprehensive web app designed to unify various college portals for an enhanced student experience at KL University. It simplifies academic tracking, hostel mess information, and faculty details into one easy-to-use platform.

How to Use KLU ONE

KLU ONE offers integrated access to multiple features. Here's a quick guide to using it effectively:

  1. ERP, LMS, SAC Portals: Use the navigation buttons in the header to seamlessly access ERP, LMS, and SAC portals.
  2. One Calculator: Access the "One Calculator" section, which includes:
    • Attendance Calculator: Enter subject names and attendance details for Lecture, Tutorial, Practical, and Skilling. Get an average attendance percentage with 90% accuracy.
    • SGPA Calculator: Input grades and credits for each subject to calculate your Semester Grade Point Average.
    • CGPA Calculator: Provide previous CGPA, previous credits, current SGPA, and current credits to calculate your Cumulative Grade Point Average.
  3. Cabin Numbers: Go to the "Faculty" section. For HODs, enter the department name or HOD name. For faculty, select the department and provide the faculty name to retrieve the cabin number.
  4. Mess Menu: Visit the "Hostel" section, select your hostel, and view the respective menu. Some deviations may occur in the menu updates.
