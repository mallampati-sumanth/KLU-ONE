About KLU ONE

KLU ONE is a unified platform designed to provide KL University students with seamless access to all university resources, portals, and tools in one convenient location. Our goal is to simplify the student experience by eliminating the need to navigate multiple websites and remember different login credentials.

With KLU ONE, you can access your ERP, LMS, academic resources, and various campus utilities through a single, user-friendly interface. We've designed this portal with student needs in mind, ensuring that you can find everything you need quickly and efficiently.

How to Use KLU ONE

  1. Navigate to Portals: Click on the "Portals" section to access ERP, LMS, and Academic resources.
  2. Explore Tools: Visit the "Tools" section for calculators, mess schedules, cabin bookings, campus maps, and transport information.
  3. Single Sign-On: Log in once to access all connected university systems without re-entering credentials.
  4. Stay Updated: Check the announcements marquee at the top for important university updates and news.
  5. Mobile Access: KLU ONE is fully responsive, allowing you to access all features from your mobile device or tablet.

About KL University

Koneru Lakshmaiah Education Foundation, commonly known as KL University, is a deemed university located in the Vaddeswaram, Guntur district of Andhra Pradesh, India. Established in 1980, KL University has grown to become one of the premier educational institutions in the country, known for its excellence in engineering, management, and sciences.

The university is committed to providing high-quality education that prepares students for successful careers and meaningful contributions to society. With state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and a focus on research and innovation, KL University offers a comprehensive learning environment that fosters academic growth and personal development.

University Achievements

  • NAAC 'A++' Grade with 3.57 CGPA
  • Ranked among Top 50 Universities in India by NIRF
  • UGC 12(B) & 2(f) Status
  • ISO 9001:2015 Certified Institution
  • Category-I University status by UGC
  • Recognized as Scientific and Industrial Research Organization (SIRO)
  • Member of Association of Indian Universities (AIU)
